Classified By: Ambassador Carol van Voorst for reasons 1.4 (b) and

(C) As Iceland's formal reply to demarche (ref B), MFA's Minister 
Counselor Nikulas Hannigan told A/Poloff on March 29 that they do not 
have the expertise or the facilities (social or security) to receive 
and integrate Guantanamo detainees.  Hannigan said Icelandic law 
provides only for accepting refugees, and these only as defined by 
UNHCR.
