NEGOTIATIONS DEMARCHE 
 
REF: SECSTATE 9584 
 
On February 1 Econoff delivered reftel demarche to Eugenia 
Llorens, Deputy Director, Spanish Civil Aviation Directorate, 
Ministry of Infrastructure ("Fomento"), and Ana de Castro, 
legal advisor to the Spanish Civil Aviation Directorate. 
Llorens and Castro are involved in coordinating the 
participation of Spain and the EU in the second stage air 
transport negotiations.  Castro called the demarche 
"helpful," and expressed optimism regarding the negotiations, 
but she had more substantive comment.
