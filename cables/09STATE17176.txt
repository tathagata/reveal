SUBJECT: NORWAY, ESTONIA, TURKEY, LUXEMBOURG, ITALY AND 
ICELAND ACCEPT ACCESSION TO NATO OF ALBANIA AND CROATIA 
 
 <a id='par1' href='#par1'>¶</a>1. (U) Norway, Estonia, Turkey, Italy, Luxembourg and 
Iceland have formally accepted the accession to NATO of 
Albania and Croatia by depositing with the U.S. Department of 
State, their instruments constituting acceptance of the NATO 
Accession Protocols. 
 
<a id='par2' href='#par2'>¶</a>2. (U) Date of deposit: Norway, Estonia, Turkey and Italy: 
February 20, 2009. Luxembourg: February 23, 2009. Iceland: 
February 24, 2009. 
 
<a id='par3' href='#par3'>¶</a>3. (U) Mission is requested to inform Allies of these 
acceptances as appropriate. 
 
<a id='par4' href='#par4'>¶</a>4. (U) These deposits bring the total to nineteen Allies that 
have completed the approval process and formally accepted the 
Protocols (Hungary, Latvia, Slovakia, Lithuania, United 
States, Bulgaria, Poland, United Kingdom, Canada, Czech 
Republic, Spain, Belgium, Denmark, Norway, Estonia, Turkey, 
Italy, Luxembourg and Iceland).  Washington will inform 
mission when further formal acceptances occur. 
 
<a id='par5' href='#par5'>¶</a>5. (U) Questions related to this procedure can be directed to 
Dmitry Vovchuk (EUR/RPM) at 202-647-2097 or Francis Holleran 
(L/T) at (202) 647-3089.
